Privacy Policy

Last updated:2026-01-25

Summary

Civics Path is designed to help you study for the U.S. Citizenship (Naturalization) civics test. We aim to minimize data collection. This policy explains what information may be processed and why.

Information We Collect

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data such as government IDs, SSNs, immigration status documents, or precise location.

  • User-provided information: If you email support, we receive the information you include (such as your email address and message).
  • App data (on your device): Study progress, saved questions, and preferences may be stored locally on your device to provide core functionality.

Analytics

    “We do not use third-party analytics SDKs."

Advertising

This app is provided with no ads. We do not sell your personal information.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve app functionality (practice tests, study tools, audio support)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Fix bugs and improve performance

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Data Retention

  • Local app data (progress, settings) remains on your device unless you delete the app or reset it.
  • Support emails are retained as long as needed to respond and maintain support history.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You can delete local app data by uninstalling the app.
  • You can contact us to request deletion of support emails you sent.
